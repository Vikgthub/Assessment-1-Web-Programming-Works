A simple yet delicious recepie, which follows just few basic essential ingredients
(cream cheese, sugar, eggs and vanilla extract) along with additional
flavourfull ingredients to enhance and enjoy the tast.
This cheesecake is a burst of chocolate flavours and can be devoured anywhere.
You can make this for a birthday, freinds, family or even enjoy the whole cheesecake by yourself!

── ⋆⋅☆ Ingredients ☆⋅⋆ ──

•┈꒰ა The Crust ໒꒱┈•

  • 1 1/2 cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs (about 10 whole graham crackers, crushed)
  • 1/4 cup (28g) confectioners' (powdered) sugar
  • 6 tablespoons (85g) butter, melted
  • 1/8 teaspoon table salt

•┈꒰ა The Filling ໒꒱┈•

  • two 8-ounce packages (452g) cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 2/3 cup (133g) sugar
  • 2 eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 cup cadburry cocoa powder
  • 1/3 cup biscoff spread
  • 1/4 cup crushed oreo cookie crumbs (any flavours)
  • 1/4 cup crushed oreo cookie filling

•┈꒰ა Decorations ໒꒱┈•

  • Whipped cream
  • White chocolate
  • crushed oreo cookie crumbs
  • kitcat chocolate
  • 1/2 cup shreded chocolate

── ⋆⋅☆ Instructions ☆⋅⋆ ──

  1. To make the crust, stir together all of the crust ingredients, mixing until thoroughly combined.
  2. Select a pie pan whose inside top dimension is at least 9", and whose height is at least 1 1/4".
  3. Then Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  4. Press the crumbs into the bottom and up the sides of the pie pan, making a thicker layer on the bottom than on the sides.
  5. To make the filling, in a bowl add the cream cheese, 2 eggs, sugarand the vanila extract, cadburry cocoa powder, biscoff spread, separated oreo cookie fillings and the crushed cookie crumbs
  6. Mix the ingredients well.
  7. Set the pie pan onto a baking sheet, if desired; this makes it easier to transport in and out of the oven, and also protects the bottom of the crust from any potential scorching. Pour the filling into the crust.
  8. To bake the cheesecake: Place the cheesecake in the oven. Bake it for 20 minutes, then add a crust shield; or shield the crust with strips of aluminum foil. Bake for an additional 10 minutes (for a total of about 30 minutes). A digital thermometer inserted into the filling 1" from the edge should read between 165°F and 170°F; the filling won't look entirely set in the center.
  9. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and set it on a rack to cool. Once the cake is cool, refrigerate it, covered, until you're ready to serve it.
  10. Decorate the cheescake with the decoations.
  11. Serve and enjoy!
